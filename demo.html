About Us

A non-ferrous foundry built for precision.

SRK Exports is an aluminium foundry in Coimbatore serving customers as a complete aluminium casting manufacturer — from pattern development through sand casting and gravity die casting, to finished, inspected, machined components. As an aluminium casting exporter, we support both low-volume prototype development and high-volume production for domestic and international buyers.

What sets us apart is being integrated: casting and machining sit under one roof, one management team, and one point of accountability — so your part moves from molten metal to finished component without changing hands between vendors.

Process / 01

Sand Casting

As a sand casting manufacturer, we produce high-quality green sand and resin sand castings for industrial applications — built for part sizes and mould flexibility that gravity die casting can't match.

Process / 02

Gravity Die Casting

As a gravity die casting supplier, our GDC line holds tighter dimensional accuracy and a cleaner as-cast surface finish, at volumes that repeat consistently, run after run.

Finishing

Castings move straight into our own machining shop — so the component that ships is finished and inspected, not just cast.

MACHINING / 01

Tool Room

In-house tool room for pattern, fixture, and jig work — so tooling changes for new or revised castings stay on our schedule, not a subcontractor's.

MACHINING / 02

Product Machining

VMC, CNC, and conventional machining bring every casting to final dimensions and surface finish, ready for assembly on arrival.
